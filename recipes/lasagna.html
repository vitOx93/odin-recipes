Lasagnas Recipe

Italian Dish

This amazing and delicious italian dish has everything you need!

Ingriedients

  • Pasta dough sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Bolognese
  • Basilikum
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Cheese, a lot of cheese..
Steps

gather all your Ingriedients

  1. preheat the oven at 180 degrees
  2. take an oven dish
  3. pour some Bechamel sauce at the bottom of the dish
  4. place a dough sheet on top of the Bechamel Sauce
  5. layer an spoon of bolognese and cheese
  6. repeat the layering of dough sheets and bolognese and cheese until desire height
  7. cover it with the last dough sheet, and pour the rest of the Bechamel over
  8. at the end, sprinkle some cheese on top and bake it for 30 minutes.
  9. let it sit and enjoy!
