Pizza Margherita

Classic Italian Dish

The most famous pizza in the world, featuring the colors of the Italian flag!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh Basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
Steps

Prepare your workspace and ingredients

  1. preheat the oven to its maximum temperature (approx. 250Â°C)
  2. stretch the dough on a floured surface
  3. spread the tomato sauce leaving the edges free
  4. add pieces of fresh mozzarella all over the surface
  5. bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden
  6. after baking, place the fresh basil leaves on top
  7. add a drizzle of olive oil and enjoy!
