Classic Tiramisu

Italian Dessert

A cold and creamy dessert that combines coffee, cocoa, and mascarpone.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers (Savoiardi)
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Eggs (yolks and whites separated)
  • White sugar
  • Strong brewed coffee (chilled)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
Steps

Make sure the coffee is cold before starting

  1. whisk the egg yolks with sugar until creamy
  2. add the mascarpone cheese and mix gently
  3. beat the egg whites until stiff and fold them into the cream
  4. dip the ladyfingers quickly into the coffee
  5. place a layer of ladyfingers in a dish and cover with cream
  6. repeat the layers until you finish the ingredients
  7. sprinkle cocoa powder on top
  8. refrigerate for at least 4 hours and enjoy!
